Living Area

La Villa Rose is a wonder for the landlords. This beautiful wooden designer house is located in the pleasant town of L'Anse-à-L'Âne, near the beach. From La Villa Rose you will enjoy beautiful views upon the Caribbean Sea and on Ramier Island. La Villa Rose has four spacious bedrooms, a large yard, and a swimming pool. La Villa Rose's unique design does not look like any other house
