La Villa Rose has a dishwasher and a washing machine. In the ktchen, La Villa Rose has everything you might need to cook and to prepare your breakfast as well as any elaborated meal.

La Villa Rose is 25-minute drive from the airport. The landlords usually rent a car at europcar-martinique.com. If you don't like to drive you can take a cab at the airport. There are shops in L'Anse-à-L'Âne, at 5-minute walk from La Villa Rose. For more elaborate shopping, use the ferry to go to Fort-de-France.

Golf afficionados will enjoy the fact that scenic Martinique Golf is 5-minute drive from La Villa Rose. Here is the link to the Golf website to get more information (in French): as-golf-martinique.com

In L'Anse-à-l'Âne there is a scuba diving club. Here is the link to their website (in French): corailclubcaraibes.com

If you enjoy horses, Le Ranch Jack is a horse club located between the golf and L'Anse-à-l'Âne. Le Ranch Jack organizes horse rides in the country or in the shade of the tropical forest. Here is the link to their website (in French): ranchjack.com

You can rent La Villa Rose anytime. The weekly rates are from 1,200 € to 1,750 €, depending on when you come and of how long you stay. Please feel free to ask any question to villa-rose.martinique@orange.fr
